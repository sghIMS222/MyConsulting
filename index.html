About Me

Sophia Hatheway is a freshman at Miami University. She is from Cincinnati, OH. Her major is Games and Simulation, and she is working towards a minor in Japanese. She graduated from Clark Montessori highschool, where she got the chance to travel to the Bahamas to study marine biology. In the summer of 2022, she took a six week canoe trip to the Hudson Bay, where she was able to visit the native reserves Kasabonika and Peawanuck. Sophia comes from a family of five. She is the eldest of three children, having two younger brothers. She is extremely happy to be furthering her studies at Miami University.
